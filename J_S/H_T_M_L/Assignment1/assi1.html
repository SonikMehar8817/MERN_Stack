About the book

It advocates the importance of financial literacy (financial education), financial independence and building wealth through investing in assets, real estate investing, starting and owning businesses, as well as increasing one's financial intelligence (financial IQ).

Why I Love It

At its core, Rich Dad Poor Dad is a book about financial education. It teaches readers how to think about money in a different way, how to build wealth, and how to achieve financial independence. But it does so in a way that is accessible, engaging, and easy to understand.

Author

Robert Kiyosaki, born on April 8, 1947, is an American entrepreneur, author, and speaker renowned for "Rich Dad Poor Dad." He emphasizes financial education, promoting the Cash Flow Quadrant concept. Kiyosaki co-founded the Rich Dad Company, offering resources on wealth creation. His teachings focus on investing, entrepreneurship, and building passive income through assets like real estate.
